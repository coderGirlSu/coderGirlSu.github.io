A B O U T   M E

Hi, I’m Su. I am currently enrolled in a standard Full Stack Developer Course at Coder Academy and currently in my first semester. Prior to Coder Academy, I had 7 yeas working experience as an Architect in China and two years of experience working as a Graphic Designer in Sydney. I always like to learn something new, so when I moved to Australia, I think it was a good opportunity to change my career. But starting over in a new country wasn't as easy as I thought, so I started learning English and studied graphic design on my own because I have 8-year painting skills and some experience with related Adobe software. That made me to decide open a small graphic design studio to start with. But as I learned more about this industry, I felt that this was not the industry I wanted to work for the rest of my life which I can enjoyed. By chance in 2021, I have a deeper understanding of the IT industry, from playing the programming game playground, to finding some free courses online to learn, making websites, I began to enjoy the sense of accomplishment that this programming brings me. From one hour of programming a day to eight hours a day, it always feels like time flies by when I'm programming. In 2022, I decided to join the Coder Academy with joy and urgency to start my IT career.
